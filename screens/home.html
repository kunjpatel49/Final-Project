Welcome to Forelook

Planning a movie night and not sure which to watch? Search for movie by their names and get critics reviews from the New York Times.
To get shoes near you with link to booking is very simple, Just enter your zipcode and all the movies avilable in your 10 mile radius will be shown to you with links to buy tickets. For Directions, Navigate to Movies Near Me section, you will get locations for all the venues with an info screen with address and directions.

You can also just click the search button and search using your current location, NO input is required!

Please note: For this feature you need to allow the app to use location services.
